Solar system is Amazing

It was formed about 4.6 billion years ago when a dense region of a molecular cloud collapsed, forming the Sun and a protoplanetary disc.

Some Planets of our Solar System

Earth, rich in life and water.

Mars, red due to iron oxide, target for exploration.

Saturn, famous for its icy rings.

Neptune, blue from methane, with stormy weather.
